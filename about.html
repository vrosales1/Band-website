MEET THE BAND

An American Rock Band formed in San Francisco in 1966 by American Guitarist, Carlos Santana. Signing with Columbia Records, the band's appearance at the WoodStock Festival in 1969 increased their profile and went on to record successful and critically-acclaimed albums. Santana is one of the best-selling groups of all time with over 47 million certified albums sold in the US, and an estimated 100 million sold worldwide.

CARLOS SANTANA

Influenced by the San Francisco Bay Area's burgeoning rock scene, in 1966 he formed the Santana Blues Band, which came to the attention of rock music impresario Bill Graham. The band began performing at the legendary club Fillmore West, and, though largely unknown, it triumphed at the Woodstock festival in 1969.The Latin influence in Santana's sounds made him "outside of the box." While he fit in with the psychedelic rock of the time, his fans were not limited to one scene or sound. He shared many influences with his contemporaries, only there was an additional current of Latin music to distinguish his rock sound.
